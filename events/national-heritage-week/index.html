National Heritage Week

Date
10 Feb 2026
Location
New Delhi

About the event

National Heritage Week highlights the value of conserving monuments, museums, archives, and living traditions. The program brings together citizens, educators, and cultural institutions through a week of public activities and learning opportunities.

Visitors can expect guided walks, expert talks, and curated sessions focused on heritage awareness and responsible tourism. The intent is to make heritage accessible while reinforcing the importance of preservation for future generations.

What to expect

Activities are designed for a broad audience, including students and families. Sessions may include short lectures, museum engagement programs, and community-led storytelling that connects local history with contemporary life.

Entry and participation details may vary by venue. For official updates and announcements, refer to the events page or contact the ministry through the official communication channels.
